Introduction

At the beginning of quarantine for the COVID-19 pandemic, I knew I wanted to create a web application with all the spare time I had. My focus was on allowing users to easily document their experience during this unprecedneted time.

I noticed a couple of weeks into quarantine, I was spending a great deal of time on my desktop. Since I didn't have a lot of lived experiences to journal, I wondered:

Could curating your web activity be the next form of journaling?
