About Me

My name is Eric Siegel and I am an aspiring UX Designer and UX Researcher from Los Angeles. I recently finished up my Cognitive Science B.S. with a minor in Statistics at the University of California, Los Angeles.

I got into UX Design/Research during my sophomore year of college. I was becoming proficient in programming, but I knew I wanted to utlize my interest in psychology and neuroscience. I found UX design and UX research, which has allowed me to utilize all of my soft skills and technical skills. I have had opportunities to do UX design and UX research work for companies such as yU+co and Logos News.

In my free time, I like to read, play guitar, take photos, and write haikus.

Please reach out if you would like to colloborate on a project, have any questions about my work, or just to say hello!
